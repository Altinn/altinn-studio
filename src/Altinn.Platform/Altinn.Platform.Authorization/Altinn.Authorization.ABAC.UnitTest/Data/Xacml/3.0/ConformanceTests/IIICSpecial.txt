Test IIIC001, 002, and 003 use hierarchical resources.

The root resource is identified by "urn:root" and 
has two child resources identified by 
"urn:root:child1" and "urn:root:child2".

The first child resource has two child resources identified by
"urn:root:child1:descendant1" and "urn:root:child1:descendant2".

The second child resource has two child resources identified by
"urn:root:child2:descendant1" and "urn:root:child2:descendant2".
